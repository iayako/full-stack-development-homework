Sunspot looking into space

Image of the Sun's chromosphere with a visible spot. This is the outer part of our star's atmosphere. This image was made from several frames and processed in Photoshop.

SkyWatcher Esprit 150 f/7 telescope, DayStar Quark Gemini lens, SkyWatcher EQ8Rh-Pro mount, ZWO ASI174MM camera, 2,000 x 16 ms.

Dugi Otok

This image, taken from an island in Croatia, shows star trails that indicate the Earth's rotation. The reflections of the stars in the water were added later because the wind prevented the photographer from capturing their actual reflection.

Nikon D600 camera, 20 mm f/2.8 lens, ISO 1600. The foreground required a shutter speed of 526 s, the sky - 247 x 25

NGC 2024 – The Flame Nebula

The Flame Nebula in the constellation Orion is located at a distance of 900 to 1500 light years from us. This is a composite image from Australia in November and December 2020.

Planewave CDK 12.5" telescope with f/8, Astrodon and Baader filters, AP900GTO mount, SBIG STXL-1100 + AOX camera, L-RGB-Ha composite, 23 hours exposure.
